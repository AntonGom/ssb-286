Who We Are

Our History

Miami Vice was founded in 1981, The founders of this place wanted to have a bar and a restuarnt at the same time while having unique and nostalgic ambience. At the time of it's founding, Miami Vice was living hrough the 80's and prospering, we had have many famous celeberties stop by and visit us and our clients right at our doorstep. In 2010, we decided to remodel the place but still keep its old retro vibe. Miami Vice is a cohesive combination between old and new.

Year Event
1981 Miami Vice was launched and opened to the public for the first time ever
1985 Miami Vice became a massive success and came in #2 in Time magazine as the best places to eat in Florida
1989 We were recognized by famous celeberties such as Madonna and David Bowie
2010 Massive Remodeling to the interior and exterior of our Bar-Restuarnt
2020 Expanded our work force and is now larger than ever with over 40 employees

Meet The Founders

Born in 1961, Tony Gomzana is an exceptional business man who is motivated by the happiness of his clients. At the age of 20 he opened up his first bussiness, Miami Vice! Although he is sometimes confused with Tony Montana due to his name and his appearance, rest assured that the only "white stuff" this Tony deals with is the wine brand we sell in our bar!

Thomas Vogel

Thomas Vogel was born in Orlando, Florida but when he was 10 years old he moved down to Miami where he later met Tony Gomzana and started to become very close friends with him. Thomas likes to play golf, travel around the world and make sure his businesses are running like a well oiled machine, if you see him in our resturant don't be shy to say hi, he loves meeting all of his clients!
